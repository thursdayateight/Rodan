We're Rodan,

an asian-gastro pub.

Food daily. DJs nightly.

And brunch. Yes, brunch.

We’re Rodan, an Asian Gastropub in the heart Wicker Park, Chicago. Ever since we opened our doors in 2004 our goal has always been to serve this city great eats, fresh beats, and comfy seats.

Every night of the week we offer dinner (‘til midnight Sun-Thurs, 1am Fri and Sat) as well as DJ’s that spin funk, soul, jazz, disco, 80’s and everything in between.

And starting Spring 2013, we’re serving up brunch - yes, brunch! - on Saturdays and Sundays from 11am-4pm.

Reservations accepted.

So come eat, lounge, dance, laugh and hang out with us.
